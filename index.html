A Society Lab @ KAIST (ASK)

A Society Lab @ KAIST (ASK) investigates varieties of social dynamics at the intersection of sociology, computational social sciences, and science and technology studies (STS). We aim to conduct rigorous research that decodes the complex dynamics of contemporary society and addresses pressing social issues by integrating empirical data with robust social theory. Grounded in the deep insights of classical and contemporary social thought, we critically reveal emergent social phenomena and analyze the underlying structures that shape them. Our lab members pursue diverse research projects on the socio-technical analysis of artificial intelligence, computational studies on political and cultural phenomena, and mixed-method approaches toward a sociology of knowledge. We achieve this by combining in-depth qualitative research and theoretical articulation with advanced computational techniques such as LLM steering and neural network analysis.
